Rides

Our tallest ride yet, Rocket 2000 takes you high in the sky and around the park at high speed!

With a lot of twists and turns, Corkscrew will definitely take you on a wild ride!

Americana takes you through record-breaking inversions for it's time when it was first built!

High Wire is a wonderful journey from high to low and even through dark tunnels!

The Wild Beast is waiting for it's next ride. Are you up for it?

Crocodile takes you through a journey similar to a crocodile chasing after prey!

Scrambler takes it's name very seriously, with gut-wrenching inversions throughout the ride!

Mono-Coaster is the latest ground-breaking coaster that takes you through a 2050s city!

Viper takes you through the jungle at high speeds, so don't think twice about getting on!

Hydro-Loop is the ultimate VR roller coaster experience as you face off foes in outerspace!

Double or Nothing breaks the record for 2 loops in a row, but are you ready to experience it?

Boardwalk High is one of the very few wooden rollercoasters at it's 100-year-operation left in existance. Ride it and help keep it at our park!

Hyper-Space isn't your typical zero gravity ride. You won't feel anything because the ride keeps you in place!

Grand X Wheel gives you a nice view of the park for those who are not able to ride our rides or need a break from walking.

Zipper 2.0 elevates the tradition ride with it's VR feature, where you'll feel like you are dangling over the tallest builing in the world!

Doppler Effect gives you the experience of those radars spinning around, but you'll be receiving high speed instead of data!

Revolution Resolution takes the frisbee ride to another level by bringing you higher and even upside down!

Mania Falls will definitely leave you soaked after zooming through tunnels to escape the Wild Beast himself!

Mania Rapids continues your journey from Mania Falls (if you rode it) through the unpredictable jungle.

Bus to Nowhere is for those young thrill seekers to go sky high at low speed!

Race Day gives young thrill seekers the opportunity to zoom around the track, but who will be the winner?

Bumble Rumble Drop will give young thrill seekers the feeling of falling without actually falling!

Buddy Spin is the fun teacup ride where young thrill seekers can ride a themed teacup with their favorite cartoon buddy!

Pilot Training will help young thrill seekers fly across the sea and deliver goods around the world!

Wild Park Carousal is our first ever ride we built long ago, and we are proud to keep this ride going for every generation!
