A Dog's Life

Are you looking for a professional and affordable dog grooming service in Mesa, Arizona? Look no further than Paws and Claws, the best place to pamper your furry friend. At A Dog's Life, we offer a full range of grooming services, including bathing, brushing, trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, teeth brushing, and more. We use only natural and gentle products that are safe for your dog's skin and coat. We also have a self-serve dog wash station where you can wash your own dog with our supplies and equipment. Our groomers are certified, experienced, and passionate about dogs. They will treat your dog with care and respect, and make sure they look and feel great. Whether you have a small or large dog, a short-haired or long-haired breed, we can handle any grooming challenge. A Dog's Life is open from Monday to Friday, from 8 am to 4 pm. You can book an appointment online or by phone, or just walk in. We also offer discounts for senior dogs, multiple dogs, and referrals. Don't wait, give your dog the gift of grooming today. Contact A Dog's Life and let us make your dog happy and healthy.
