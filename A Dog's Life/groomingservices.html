Grooming Services

A Dog's Life is a dog shop that offers a variety of dog grooming services for your furry friends. Some of the services they provide are:

  • Bath and Brush: This service includes a shampoo, conditioner, blow dry, brush out, nail trim, ear cleaning, and anal gland expression. It is suitable for dogs of all sizes and breeds, and helps to keep their coat clean and healthy. The price ranges from $25 to $65, depending on the size and condition of the dog.
  • Haircut: This service includes everything in the Bath and Brush service, plus a haircut of your choice. You can choose from different styles, such as teddy bear, lion, schnauzer, poodle, etc. The haircut service is ideal for dogs with long or curly hair that need regular trimming. The price ranges from $45 to $95, depending on the size and condition of the dog.
  • Teeth Cleaning: This service includes a thorough brushing of your dog's teeth with a special toothpaste and toothbrush, followed by a rinse and a breath freshener. Teeth cleaning helps to prevent plaque, tartar, and bad breath, and improves your dog's oral health. The price is $10 per dog.
  • De-Shedding: This service includes a special shampoo and conditioner that help to loosen and remove the undercoat, followed by a blow dry and a de-shedding tool that removes the excess hair. De-shedding reduces shedding by up to 90%, and makes your dog's coat softer and shinier. The price ranges from $15 to $35, depending on the size and condition of the dog.
  • Spa Package: This service includes everything in the Haircut service, plus a teeth cleaning, a de-shedding treatment, a blueberry facial, a pawdicure, and a bandana or bow. The spa package is the ultimate pampering experience for your dog, and leaves them looking and feeling great. The price ranges from $75 to $125, depending on the size and condition of the dog.

To book an appointment for any of these services, you can call A Dog's Life at (480) 555-1234. They are open from Monday to Saturday, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. They are located at 123 Main Street, Mesa, AZ 85201.
